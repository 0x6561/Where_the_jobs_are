Email Data Mining
I have been looking for a job, and as a result I have posted my resume in a variety of places and indicated that I am seeking employment in Phoenix Arizoan or Remote and I have received several inquiries/proposals for various roles. It seemed like most of these offers were for other places so decided to conduct a bit of data mining of my email to either prove or disprove this theory. I used Python and a bit of natural language processing and D3JS to visualize the results.
